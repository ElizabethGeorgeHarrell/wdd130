Overview

Purpose

The creation of this website for Rafting Momentum is to inform customers about products, good rafting locations, and to introduce newcomers to river rafting. It's also a good place to schedule rafting lessons and tours. tours and routes,

Audience

The audience for Rafting Momentum is targeted towards beginners in water rafting. The website will not only include supplies and equipment, but also contact information of instructors, and detailed guides to each path and river, so that beginners can feel safe and prepared. The website is especially targeted to kids and teenagers in an effort to cultivate life-lasting buisness and more interest from younger generations in outdoor sports.

Branding

The branding for the site will be symplistic, and will emphasize the sports gear aspect of the products.
